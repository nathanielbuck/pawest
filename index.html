Risk Management and Clearances

On January 1, 2015 the changes in the PA Child Protective Services Act went into effect. These changes are a dramatic change from the previous version of the law and require we all make changes to how we approach protecting the children in our organizations. PA West Soccer can no longer provide a clearance through a third party source and therefore has discontinued offering an eKidsafe clearance. We have made adjustments to our system so it will help us manage the documents required by the new law.

Please read this document from Tim McCoy on how the Pennsylvania law changes affect PA West referees: Risk Management Changes.

Unanchored Goals

It's referee's responsibility to make sure players are safe. The first priority when arriving at a field is determining if it's fit to play on. Failing to perform this duty is a significant breach of the Referees's responsiblies, and could result in players getting injured.

If goals are not anchored, do not start the game!
